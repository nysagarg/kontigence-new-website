Why should I register?

It's completely free

We are doing this to make everyone's life better at this hour, and we're going to do it for free. All it takes is willpower and some time!

Kill some free time

With the quarantine, it is best to stay home as much as possible. Let's make your quarantined days interesting.

Meet new people

For all the extroverts stuck at home, this is the perfect opportunity to meet new people, who have unique talents.

Certified appreciation

We know how good it feels to be appreciated, and we will be giving certificates to all our participants and merit competitors.

Learn a new skill

There's no downside to learning. Who knows, perhaps something you learn in Kontigence may help you take important decisions in the future!

How do I register?

Individual or School?

We divided registrations into two parts- individual and institutional. This is to streamline the registration process. So decide if you are coming as a delegation from a school, or as individual participants.

Decide and detail

We have a lot of options- so decide what you want to participate in and fill the appropriate form linked below. We would appreciate any non-interested viewers to not spam us, we are trying our best to make this time enjoyable, and any support is appreciated.

Registration

Kontigence is a one-of-a-kind experience which will not only make your quarantine much more fun, but also expose you to a brand new platform and amazing people who have the same interests as you.

We know this isn't an amazing time, but let's make it better.

Spread the word

Now that you've filled in the registration form, we'll do our part and confirm your registration soon.
