Letter to the Participants<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Dear Participants,<\/p>

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you to Kontigence – The Trader. Financial literacy is a must known skill in the 21st<\/sup> century that is highly undervalued. The older generation has made money seem like an evil commodity that only greedy people desire. This is not true!<\/p>

Of course, money may not be everything man desires, but the desire for monetary wellness does not make one selfish. It is important to understand the value of money and investments, and how over time, good investments lead to good returns. Kontigence wishes to provide a platform where youngsters can learn more about the working of finances closely in a fun and educational manner.<\/p>

This event is targeted at scholars who despite being a young age wish to be financially educated and to get an insight into the real corporate world, where money is \u201cgrown\u201d i.e a stock exchange. <\/span><\/p>

Good Luck!<\/p>

Team Kontigence<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Guidelines<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Participants would be required to participate individually or in teams of two.<\/p>

A virtual trading platform \u201cTradingKart<\/a>\u201d will be used where individual accounts will be made for each participant. The goal is simple: \u201cGrow your money\u201d! The participant who has the maximum portfolio value at the end of two days is the winner.<\/span><\/p>

We encourage the participants to try the platform before the event. You can do so by making a free new account. A starting amount of\u00a0$1,000,000 will be given to all participants.<\/p>

For the competition, an account generated by our team will be sent to all participants. Only that account is to be used for trading. Pre-existing accounts will not be allowed to compete and will result in immediate disqualification of the participant.<\/p>

Note:<\/strong> For this competition we will be using the US Stock Market Exchange (NYSE & NASDAQ) which opens at different times for all timezones. This means that this event will take place apart from the timings of other events and you can trade at your own pace. Since this does not disrupt with any other Kontigence event, you are free to participate in other events also.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
