Letter to the Participants<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Dear Gamers,<\/span><\/p>

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you to Kontigence.\u00a0<\/p>

In the stressed file life of today, with global pandemics and possibilities of world wars surrounding us, it is okay to take a break. To pick up your controller and roam through deserted establishments, ridding the world of evils. Gaming is a competition to some, and to some, it is a feeling, of being able to do anything. We invite everyone, to participate in the gaming showdown of your lifetime.<\/p>

Today, you don’t have to pick up your controller or log into your Steam account to be a hero- you can do so by staying at home, self-quarantined, in an attempt to flatten the curve. We call all heroes- with our without capes, to game on, to infinity and beyond.<\/p>

We provide you with an avenue to utilise your hand-eye coordination to express yourself in an inspiring manner using video games. Using a number of metrics and games, the highest scorer takes the title of Kontingence’s Greatest Gamer, and a lot of added satisfaction.<\/p>

Game on!<\/p>

Team Kontigence<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

General Guidelines<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

The player allocation for the stages will be based on the order of registration, and accordingly the time scheduling will be shared with all participants.<\/p>

If any form of hacking or other unethical actions are detected, you will be instantly disqualified from all Kontigence events, and your institution will be notified about your lack of integrity.<\/p>

This is an individual competition, so no unauthorized teaming is permitted. All participants must have a functional steam account as the games will be available for download there. It is expected that everyone will have compared their computer\u2019s specifications and ensured they are sufficient to run the games.<\/p>

The individual rankings for each round will be shared along with all participants. The participant with the highest total points across each event will be declared as the Kontigence Greatest Gamer Champion.<\/span><\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

Round 1: CSGO<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Can you prove yourself to be the best at tactical strategies and marksmanship? This globally recognized game is available for free on the steam store. Server details will be shared with the participants in advance. Points will be allocated based on the number of kills in a limited time free for all, and each player gets one chance to play in an official match, so prepare well.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

Round 2: Brawlhalla<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

This game is based on the massively popular concept of super smash bros. It is also available for free on the steam store. Server room details will be shared with the participants in advanced. The gamemode will be classic last man standing, with 2 lives per competitor and a limited time of 10 minutes per round. All participants will get one chance to play in an official match, and points will be allocated on the basis of rank and knockouts.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t

Round 3: Wildcard<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

The final round will be a mystery game which will only be announced after the conclusion of the third game. It will also be free and available on steam, but shall differ from the themes of the other two rounds.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
