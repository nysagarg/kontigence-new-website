Letter to the Participants<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Welcome creatives,<\/p>

Today, where Artificial Intelligence and machines are replacing humans in terms of raw workforce, abstract, unquantifiable skills like creativity and ingenuity have become more important than ever. To sharpen your blades of creativity and test your skills, we have an exciting event called The influencer lined up for you.<\/p>

We welcome you to an event where the only limit is your creativity, so unleash your potential in this photo-editing contest. In a world with increasing fake news, it is indeed important to be able to differentiate between what’s correct and what’s not- and editing some photos, and looking at samples will allow you to understand how information is often twisted and manipulated before being broadcasted.<\/p>

And learning a skill never goes to waste. You may not decide to be a professional graphic designer, but being familiar with the potential of Photoshop will always come handy, and that’s something we can guarantee. We understand that this going to be new for a lot of you, and we welcome all new-comers to this one-of-a-kind experience.<\/p>

Happy Designing!<\/p>

Team Kontigence<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Guidelines<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

To ensure a competition as fair as possible, we aim to provide even footing for all participating competitors. Due to the same, Kontigence will provide a base image which all participants will perform edits on. Participants may use parts and elements from other images as long as the base image is identifiable after the edits have been performed. The image will be chosen as per the discretion of the Kontigence and can belong to any category- landscape, portraits, abstract and more.\u00a0<\/span>Furthermore, the image provided will be a high-quality image in order to ease any pixel-edge detection.<\/span><\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Submission<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Before submission, make sure you register yourself for this event.<\/strong><\/p>

The submissions for this event are open till\u00a0Friday the 22nd<\/sup>\u00a0of May at 12pm GMT<\/strong>. The evaluation of any submission after the deadline is at Team Kontigence’s discretion.<\/p>

We require you to submit the files at our email address-\u00a0submissions@Kontigence.com<\/a><\/p>

Please ensure that the subject of your email is in the following format:<\/p>

[The influencer] Your Name, School Name<\/strong><\/p>

You will receive a confirmation email in 24 hours of submission. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please contact us.<\/p>

All participants are to submit the .psd file with edits and the final, exported .jpg file to the Kontigence email address. Any incomplete or late submission will automatically be disqualified. If the participants use any other software to edit, they will have to submit the raw, editable file in the native file format of that particular software. The time allotted for the participants to perform edit will be revealed soon. Stay tuned!t<\/span><\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Scoring Metric<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

For fair evaluation, participants\u2019 entries will be judged on the basis of the criteria listed below.<\/p>

Degree of realism – <\/strong>How realistic the image looks<\/p>

Creativity –<\/strong> How innovative the edits are<\/span><\/p>

Complexity – <\/strong>How difficult the edits are to perform well<\/span><\/p>

Uniqueness –<\/strong> How different is the image compared to other participants<\/p>

Each of these 4 categories will be graded out of 5, for a total of 20.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
