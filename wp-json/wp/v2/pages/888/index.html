Letter to the Participants<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Welcome creatives,<\/p>

Representation is an important aspect of life, and the saying “The first impression is the final impression” is true to quite an extent in the world of marketing. To offer all participants an insight into the same, we pitch Kontigence Vector Wars.<\/p>

An ultimate showdown of creatives from every corner, Vector Wars aims to instil a fundamental understanding of graphic designing and its various intricacies. We also aim to make people familiar with vector-editing softwares, which are amazing tools with several applications in the daily life.<\/p>

Art is a form of expression, and we want to see you express who you are, and what you think like. We want your artworks to be a reflection of who you are, which makes every participant a winner. If you love drawing or have tried out graphic design in the past, this is the perfect opportunity for you! Read further to know more.<\/p>

Happy Designing!<\/p>

Team Kontigence<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Guidelines<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

To ensure a competition as fair as possible, we aim to provide even footing for all participating competitors. Due to the same, Kontigence will provide a topic to all participants which will test the creativity of each designer. Participants will be given 24 hours to create one illustration, and they may include any stylistic device provided that the final graphic remains appropriate to all demographics. Puns and creative spin-offs relating to the topic will be appreciated.<\/span><\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Submission<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Before submission, make sure you register yourself for this event.<\/strong><\/p>

The submissions for this event are open till\u00a0Friday the 22nd<\/sup>\u00a0of May at 12pm GMT<\/strong>. The evaluation of any submission after the deadline is at Team Kontigence’s discretion.<\/p>

We require you to submit the .svg file at-\u00a0submissions@Kontigence.com<\/a><\/p>

Please ensure that the subject of your email is in the following format:<\/p>

[Vector Wars] Your Name, School Name<\/strong><\/p>

You will receive a confirmation email in 24 hours of submission. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please contact us.<\/p>

All participants are to submit the svg<\/strong> file to the Kontigence email address. Regardless of which software the participants use, the final file has to strictly be an svg (Scalable Vector Graphics) file. Submissions in any other file format will not be judged and be discarded.<\/span><\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

Scoring Metric<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

For fair evaluation, participants\u2019 entries will be judged on the basis of the criteria listed below.<\/p>

Creativity –<\/strong> How innovative the edits are<\/span><\/p>

Complexity – <\/strong>How difficult the edits are to perform well<\/span><\/p>

Uniqueness –<\/strong> How different is the image compared to other participants<\/p>

Each of these 3 categories will be graded out of 10, for a total of 30.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
