Letter to the Participants<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Dear Scholars,<\/p>

It gives us immense pleasure to welcome you to Kontigence – Politically Incorrect.<\/p>

It is said that one person with a strong belief in their ideology is more powerful than twenty people with a thought. An ideology is ideas, ideals, doctrines, beliefs, myths or symbols of a community and a large group that explains how society should work. Individuals with strong beliefs in their ideologies have caused many life altering events in the history of the world. Wars, revolution, 9\/11, Sandy Hook and other various life-changing events such as The Civil Rights Movement and LBGTQ pride.\u00a0Regardless of if the events were considered good or bad, they occurred due to the individuals\u2019 beliefs and thought processing.<\/p>

Ideologies also offer a political and cultural blueprint for a desired social order. Ideology is the basis of \u201cpublic opinion\u201d or common sense; which usually remains invisible to most people within society. Some examples of ideologies are socialism, liberalism, conservatism, and religion. Ideologies are neither right or wrong. The meaning of ideology is often debated and political scientists cannot agree on whether ideology is a positive, negative, or neutral feature in modern society. Although political scientists have not come to an agreement on an exact definition for ideology, their opinions support the idea that political ideologies are usually simply stated and oriented. The increased polarization in ideologies over time has caused a rift has been created between the left and the right, and liberals and conservatives. This event offers you a platform to express your ideology in form of a discussion against someone of the opposite ideology.<\/p>

Let’s get Debating!<\/p>

Team Kontigence<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t

General Guidelines<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

A political topic based international events would be given to the participants, on which they would be required to have a 10-15 minute discussion with a moderator on a live call. The topic will be given 30 minutes before the call. The moderator would stimulant the participant with additional information, or provide a rebuttal to their opinion.<\/p>

The moderator’s job is not to score the discussion, rather facilitate the expression of opinions by the participants.<\/p>

Either perspective regarding the topic would be equality respected by the judges and no one will be penalized for their views. The scope of this even is to see how well the views are presented rather than what they are. Hence, The perspective of the participant would not influence the scoring.<\/p>

Kontigence is meant to be a Covid-19 relief for highschool students; hence, the topic for this event not be related to<\/strong> COVID-19<\/strong>.<\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
