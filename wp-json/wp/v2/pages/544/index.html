Letter to the Participants\n<\/h4>\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t
\n\t\t\t\t\t

Dear Participants,<\/p>\n

We are honoured to welcome you to Kontingence’s Standup Comedy- an opportunity to outshine your competitors with skilful mastery of website development. We expect this event to serve as a pinnacle of creativity and innovation, with functionality and aesthetics as fundamental pillars of support.<\/p>\n

In a world where online marketing is reaching the pinnacle of influence, it is important to be able to represent an organisation or yourself as an individual. A website is an easy-to-access medium of information, and through this event, we aim to inculcate basic, if not advanced, web developing skills. We put forward the Standup Comedy as a stage of talent and ingenuity, hoping to see participants dive into the world of marketing and brand presentation.<\/span><\/p>\n

This occasion is explicitly structured as a stage for creative individuals with the focal spotlight on helping them improve their insight and sharpen their abilities. There are of course, certain factors that one should keep in mind while enrolling in this event. While some experience would definitely be helpful, we encourage everyone to come and learn something new!<\/p>\n

May the deserving participants win!<\/p>\n

Team Kontigence<\/span><\/p><\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t\t<\/div>\n\t\t<\/section>\n\t\t\t\t
