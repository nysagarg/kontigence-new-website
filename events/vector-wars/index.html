Vector Wars

Participants will be given a theme or a topic upon which they will have to create a certain number of vector artworks in a set duration of time. 

Letter to the Participants

Welcome creatives,

Representation is an important aspect of life, and the saying “The first impression is the final impression” is true to quite an extent in the world of marketing. To offer all participants an insight into the same, we pitch Kontigence Vector Wars.

An ultimate showdown of creatives from every corner, Vector Wars aims to instil a fundamental understanding of graphic designing and its various intricacies. We also aim to make people familiar with vector-editing softwares, which are amazing tools with several applications in the daily life.

Art is a form of expression, and we want to see you express who you are, and what you think like. We want your artworks to be a reflection of who you are, which makes every participant a winner. If you love drawing or have tried out graphic design in the past, this is the perfect opportunity for you! Read further to know more.

Happy Designing!

Team Kontigence

Guidelines

To ensure a competition as fair as possible, we aim to provide even footing for all participating competitors. Due to the same, Kontigence will provide a topic to all participants which will test the creativity of each designer. Participants will be given 24 hours to create one illustration, and they may include any stylistic device provided that the final graphic remains appropriate to all demographics. Puns and creative spin-offs relating to the topic will be appreciated.

Submission

Before submission, make sure you register yourself for this event.

The submissions for this event are open till Friday the 22nd of May at 12pm GMT. The evaluation of any submission after the deadline is at Team Kontigence’s discretion.

We require you to submit the .svg file at- submissions@Kontigence.com

Please ensure that the subject of your email is in the following format:

[Vector Wars] Your Name, School Name

You will receive a confirmation email in 24 hours of submission. If you do not receive a confirmation email, please contact us.

All participants are to submit the svg file to the Kontigence email address. Regardless of which software the participants use, the final file has to strictly be an svg (Scalable Vector Graphics) file. Submissions in any other file format will not be judged and be discarded.

Scoring Metric

For fair evaluation, participants’ entries will be judged on the basis of the criteria listed below.

Creativity – How innovative the edits are

Complexity – How difficult the edits are to perform well

Uniqueness – How different is the image compared to other participants

Each of these 3 categories will be graded out of 10, for a total of 30.

Sample Material

A sample vector file can be previewed and compared below, which would give you an idea of the kind of creativity you can unleash at this platform. The theme for the sample image is “sample material”. More examples of vector images can be found throughout the Kontigence website. It is important to note that you are only limited by your creativity, as Kontigence will ensure that ample time is provided to all.

Topic for Vector Artwork

Each participant has to illustrate a city as aesthetically as possible. They may choose to include the most identifiable parts of a city in their illustration. Each designer may select their own city they want to illustrate.
